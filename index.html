Why Us




  • 1. We will save your money

  • 2. We rent Rooms quicker

  • 3. We could increase your income

  • 4. Based in South London

We charge 6% inc VAT with no hidden costs. Most letting agents advertise a fee, for example 10%, but when you add their extra charges plus VAT, the net cost to you is much more.

Due to the tenant fee ban most letting agents are now charging landlords for simple paperwork. Add VAT to this and your net cost will not be 10% but more like 14%.

We are a specialist HMO letting agent. We know what rents, where to advertise and we have a database of thousands of professionals who are seeking good quality rooms.

Your target market is millennials, and marketing to millennials is a skill in of itself. We understand their values and their lifestyle expectations. It’s important we match the right people with the right HMO.

We will rent your HMO room by room, when doing this you’ll be maximising your returns. Most letting agents just try and find one large group of sharers, this decreases your income.

Other agents won’t even entertain the idea of renting your HMO room by room. They will just upload your property to Rightmove and Zoopla as they don’t know how to effectively market HMOs.

We will normally have a HMO that we manage not too far from your property. We know the hot and cold areas in South London and which areas attract which type of tenant.

Every day we are on the road in South London and we would love to come and meet you today. Contact us and find out if we can increase your rental income and save you money of fees.
